CBSE Exam Rules & Instructions

  • Students must carry their Admit Card to the examination center.
  • Reach the exam hall at least 30 minutes before the start time.
  • Electronic gadgets like mobile phones, smartwatches, and calculators are strictly prohibited.
  • Students must bring their own stationery. Borrowing during the exam is not allowed.
  • Any unfair means will lead to disqualification.
  • Follow instructions given by invigilators strictly.
  • Attempt all questions neatly and legibly.
  • Fill in the answer sheet carefully — especially roll number, subject code, etc.
